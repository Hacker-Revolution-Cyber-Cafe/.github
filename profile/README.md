## 🔒 Our Core Pillars

### ⚡ Ethical Hacking & Offensive Security
We identify vulnerabilities before they can be exploited. Our team specializes in:
* **Web & Mobile Pentesting:** Deep analysis of applications to prevent data breaches.
* **Vulnerability Assessment (SAST/DAST):** Integration of security testing into the development lifecycle.
* **Server Hardening:** Strengthening infrastructure against advanced persistent threats.

### 🛠️ Custom Software Development
We build software with security baked in from the first line of code:
* **Tailored Solutions:** Specialized systems designed for complex business logic.
* **Secure APIs:** Robust integration hubs with advanced encryption and authentication.
* **Legacy Modernization:** Upgrading existing systems with a focus on performance and security.

### ⚖️ Compliance & Governance
Helping businesses navigate the complexity of data protection laws:
* **LGPD/GDPR Adequacy:** Ensuring data privacy and legal compliance.
* **Information Security Policy (ISP):** Developing protocols to protect corporate assets.
